How New Era Structures Works

We provide confidence for when you need to purchase a custom carport from us, not only will your property be protected, but your needs will be met without breaking the bank. We have the products you want, service you expect, and prices you can afford!

Quote

We first recieve the information of the building you inquired for. Then we explain the details of how our company works to build the carport, barn, garage, or building for you.

Send information of your inquiry

Second, we send you the quote to your phone or email with a design of the building you requested. New Era uses all the technology possible to make sure we show you what you requested.

Send order to manufacture 

The third step, we send the order to the manufacture that is closest to your state. Our lead time is less than 3 weeks and you'll be notified when your building is done.
