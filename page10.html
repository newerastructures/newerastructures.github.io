THREE ROOF STYLES

  1. REGULAR - curved ends with sheeting running lengthwise. The A-shaped is distinctly known from this style and can be offered with a variety of colors and sizes.
  2. BOXED-EAVE - has sheeting that also runs lengthwise and is flat all the way down. It’s V-shape is most widely known on its edges and can also be offered in different colors and sizes.
  3. VERTICAL ROOF - is shaped exactly as the boxed-eave along its edges, but it’s sheeting runs vertically.
At New Era Structures, you can find a variety of metal carports to order including metal garages, commercial structures, and custom buildings.
